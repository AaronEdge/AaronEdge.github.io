About

I believe that games are a powerful medium and that we have a lot to learn from the psychological effect which they can have. The same traits that make a game compelling to play can be used to make other daily tasks just as appealing.

I am a software developer with a keen interest in gamification. I apply these interests to make applications that people love to use.

I have a unique blend of skills which lends itself to the creation of software that is built to excite people. My creative design background helps to bring an emotional connection to the software I develop, bringing it to life.
